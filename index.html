// how engagements work

The engagement log

Every engagement follows the same release cycle. No surprise scope creep, no vague "strategy phase" that never ships.

  1. v0.1

    Discovery

    Two weeks. A full audit of your stack, team, and roadmap. No assumptions carried over from the last engineer who left.

  2. v0.4

    Assessment

    A prioritized, unvarnished list of what's broken, what's risky, and what can genuinely wait.

  3. v1.0

    Roadmap

    A plan your team can execute without you sitting in every single meeting.

  4. v1.x

    Execution

    Ongoing fractional leadership on a weekly or monthly cadence, tuned to what the team needs right now.

  5. v2.0

    Handoff

    A full-time hire, an internal promotion, or a continued partnership. Your roadmap, your call.
